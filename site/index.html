H1
Correct acquisition
Correct-choice probability rises above its frozen floor in all three confirmed model families.

          
H2
Planted learning
Negative controls raise the planted target more than the intended target—not merely “do nothing.”

          
H3
Temporal necessity
Earlier residual states increasingly suppress the late checkpoint’s correct answer in a coherent depth band.

          
H4
Sample corruption
Dirty-name states patched into clean-name prompts suppress probability specifically for the clean rule.
